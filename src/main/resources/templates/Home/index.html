Organizations

The Office Planner Scheduling System is meant to serve Tracom Services Limited ease meeting planning together with their immediate neighbouring organizations such as Pergamon Group, Right Here, Hudaco e.t.c with whom they share resources such as meeting rooms and parking spaces.

Solution

Scheduling made easy! What do we mean by that?
Office Planner provides a platform to keep record of meetings ahead of time as well as manage conflicts between schedules by presenting current meeting room schedules. Users get access on their devices either on mobile or web.

Notifications

Employees and attendees get notified of the meeting schedules via email and or SMS and get reminded of 15 minutes prior to the incoming meeting start time.
